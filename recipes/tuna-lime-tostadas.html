Tuna Lime Tostadas

Ingredients:

  • 15 ounces of canned white tuna (drained)
  • 5 ounces of canned sweet corn (drained)
  • Half an onion, finely chopped
  • 1 bunch of cilantro, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp salsa
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • Hot sauce (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Sour cream
  • 8 tostada shells

Steps:

  1. Place tuna in a bowl and flake with fork
  2. Mix tuna with corn, onion, cilantro, salsa, lime juice, hot sauce, salt, and pepper
  3. Spoon filling onto tostada shells
  4. Top tostadas with sour cream and hot sauce (optional)
