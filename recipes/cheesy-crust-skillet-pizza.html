Cheesy Crust Skillet Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 and a half cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 5 thinly sliced cherry tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons of torn fresh basil leaves
  • 4 thinly sliced mozzarella balls

Steps:

  1. Heat a 10-inch non-stick skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese into the skillet for 2-3 minutes, or until melted
  3. Arrange tomatoes, basil leaves, and mozzarella slices on the cheese
  4. Cook for 2-3 minutes, or until top is bubbling and edges are browned
  5. Remove pizza from the skillet
  6. Cool for 1 minute before slicing
  7. Enjoy!
