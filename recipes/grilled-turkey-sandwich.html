Grilled Turkey and Swiss Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp mayo
  • 2 slices of rye bread
  • 2 slices of Swiss cheese
  • 2 slices of turkey meat
  • 1/4 cup of baby spinach

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven broiler
  2. Spread mayo onto 1 side of each slice of bread
  3. Layer cheese, turkey, and spinnach onto one slice of bread
  4. Place other slice of bread on top
  5. Broil until cheese bubbles (about 5 mins)
  6. Enjoy!
